More About Rachel

Here you'll find more detail about my academic path, research interests, awards, and a glimpse into my life outside the lab.

Background

I was born and raised in San Diego, California to Chinese-Vietnamese immigrant parents. As a first-generation scholar, I’ve always been motivated by the opportunities education and research can open, both for myself and for others. My path has taken me from sunny Southern California to the charming East Coast, where I’m currently pursuing my Ph.D. at MIT.
